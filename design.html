LOGO

OVERVIEW

CSL Eventtechnik is a local event technology service provider in Ingolstadt. As it was my first personal project, I faced considerable challenges. Not even knowing how Adobe Illustrator works, was the most difficult parts. I really wanted to realize my customer's idea and came up with the shown above result.

TIME

Feb 2021 - April 2021

TOOLS

PROCESS

How I designed this logo:

  • Step 1: Choosing a font

    The first thing I did was searching for a sans-serif font with a very modern and technical look. Finally I came up with sans-serif font "Good Times".

  • Step 2: Designing a figurative mark

    I symbols which match an event technology service provider, I came across an equalizer panel. What would better fit than these light bars on an equalizer? - Probably nothing.

  • Step 3: Choosing colors

    I wanted to use the original colors blue and red, but in a different shade. Using them within a linear gradient on the figurative mark, I created before, seemed to be the best solution.

  • Step 4: Positioning and Sizing

    With a bigger and bolder style, matching the height of the light bars, the "CSL" is standing out a little bit more. I like this fact, because the three letters are the customers' initials. Below I placed "Eventtechnik" in a lighter font, but with the same width as the upper part, to resemble a rectangle.

OVERVIEW

Besides my studies I work voluntarily for an association called N.I.C.E. N.I.C.E. is a student initiation that aims to bring the international and German students together for Networking and International Culture Exchange. One year ago, I joined the marketing departement and started creating the event posters.

How Iâ€™m usually designing event posters:

  • Step 1: Searching for Inspiration

    Usually I'm searching for posters by the events' name. I try to find a style I like and think it fits to my topic perfectly.

  • Step 2: Jumping into Illustrator

    After creating an artboard in DIN-A1 format, I think about a possible choice of colors.

  • Step 3: Choosing matching colors

    In advance I create a color palette, to see if my imagined colors match together. During the process, I do sometimes recognize, that I've chosen too many colors. Then I reduce the amount of different colors.

  • Step 4: Adding the logo and relevant information

    The most important information, the title is in focus. Further information is placed right next to it. The logo is always placed in the right bottom corner.

  • Step 5: Positioning and Sizing

    Depending on how relevant an information seems to me, I adjust the font-size and -weight. With this step I'm finalizing my posters and send them to the Marketings head to check if all the information is correct.
